ABOUT THIS PROJECT

The Purpose of This Website

Welcome to our official section website! I created this platform as a digital scrapbook to collect and preserve our happy memories as students of Grade 7 Paterno. Please note that this project is made purely for entertainment purposes and to give us something to look back on in the future.

I would also like to take this opportunity to sincerely apologize if there are any profile pictures or images here that you might not like. The pictures I used were simply found online and cropped to fit the design layout. It is never my intention to embarrass anyone or cause any discomfort.

If you wish to change your displayed photo, you can easily do so. Just send me your preferred formal picture, and I will update it right away. Furthermore, I am very open to suggestions. If you have any ideas, want to add new features, or have specific photos and videos you want to be included in our gallery, please do not hesitate to reach out.

Drop your photos or suggestions here:
devxdave1@gmail.com

Ang Layunin ng Website na Ito

Maligayang pagdating sa ating official section website! Ginawa ko ang platform na ito upang magsilbing digital na tambayan at koleksyon ng ating mga masasayang alaala bilang mga mag-aaral ng Grade 7 Paterno. Ang proyektong ito ay ginawa purely for entertainment purposes lamang at para may mabalikan tayong magagandang memories sa hinaharap.

Nais ko ring humingi ng paumanhin kung mayroon mang mga larawan na hindi ninyo nagustuhan. Ang mga pictures na ginamit dito ay nakita ko lamang at nai-crop para magkasya sa ating layout. Wala akong intensyon na mapahiya o maging discomfort ang sinuman sa inyo.

Kung gusto ninyong palitan ang inyong profile picture, madali lang 'yan. Magpadala lang kayo ng inyong paboritong formal picture sa akin at papalitan ko agad ito. Bukas din ako sa anumang suggestions—kung may gusto kayong idagdag na features, impormasyon, o mga bagong larawan at videos sa ating gallery, sabihin niyo lang sa akin.
